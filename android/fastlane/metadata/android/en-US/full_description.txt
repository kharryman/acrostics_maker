Acrostics maker is a tool to help create acrostics for names such that names themselves become acronyms. It is useful for remembering the key qualities of things.
One example is the word 'aardvark' or a single species of nocturnal African anteaters ho dig for ants. It can be represented as:
African
Ants
Ravenous
Digging
Vampire
Alone
Represented
Kingdom
